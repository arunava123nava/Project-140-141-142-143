AI Ping Pong Game











Developed By:Arunava Dhar.

Instructions

  1. First Keep Your Laptop Screen Straight
  2. Move Your Self Approximately 3 To 4 Feet Away From The Laptop
  3. Move Your Right Wrist In Ascending And Descending Manner, A Red Dot Should Appear On Your Right Wrist
  4. Now Press The Play Button, And Again Move Your Self 3 To 4 Feet Away From Laptop
  5. The Red Paddel Is Your Paddel, That Will Move As Per The Movement Of Your Right Wrist
